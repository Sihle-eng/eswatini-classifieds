Safety Tips for Buyers & Sellers

Your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a smooth and secure experience on Eswatini Classifieds.

For Buyers

  • Check the seller's reputation: Look at their profile and any reviews left by others.
  • Ask lots of questions: Request additional photos, proof of ownership, or service history.
  • Never pay in advance: Avoid sending money via e‑wallet or bank transfer without seeing the item first.
  • Meet in a safe location: Choose a busy, public place – e.g., a shopping mall, petrol station, or police station.
  • Bring a friend: It's always safer to have someone with you.
  • Inspect the item thoroughly: Test electronics, check vehicle VIN numbers, and verify documents.
  • Use cash or secure methods: Cash is simple, but for high amounts, consider a bank transfer done on the spot.

For Sellers

  • Be honest in your listing: Describe any defects accurately to avoid disputes.
  • Price fairly: Research similar items to set a competitive price.
  • Never share personal info: Only give your phone number after the buyer shows genuine interest.
  • Meet in a public place: Avoid inviting strangers to your home.
  • Check the money: Count cash carefully and watch for counterfeit notes.
  • Transfer ownership properly: For vehicles, ensure all paperwork is signed and a copy is kept.
  • Remove your ad promptly: Once sold, mark it as sold to avoid further enquiries.

Red Flags – Watch Out!

  • Overpaying and asking for a refund of the difference.
  • Pressure to act quickly or use unconventional payment methods.
  • Refusal to meet in person or provide additional photos.
  • Vague answers or inconsistent information.

If something feels off, trust your instincts and walk away. Report suspicious behaviour to us.

What We Do to Keep You Safe

  • We verify user emails and monitor activity for unusual patterns.
  • We provide a reporting system for scams or inappropriate content.
  • We regularly update our safety guides and community standards.
