How It Works

Getting started on Eswatini Classifieds is simple. Whether you're selling an item or looking to buy, follow these steps to make the most of our platform.

1

Create an Account or Log In

If you're new, sign up for a free account using your email or phone. If you already have an account, simply log in. Having an account lets you post ads, save favourites, and manage your listings.

2

Post Your Ad

Click the “Post Ad” button. Fill in the title, description, category, price (if applicable), location, and upload one or more images. The more detail you provide, the quicker you'll find a buyer.

  • Use clear, high‑quality photos.
  • Be honest about the condition and price.
  • Include contact info (phone or email) – but only share it after an initial enquiry for privacy.
3

Connect with Interested Buyers

Once your ad is live, buyers will contact you via the phone number or email you provided. Reply promptly, answer questions honestly, and negotiate a fair price.

Pro tip: Keep your ad active by renewing it every 30 days – we'll remind you!

4

Close the Deal Safely

Arrange to meet in a public, well‑lit place – like a shopping centre or police station. Bring a friend if possible. Inspect the item carefully before paying, and count cash together. For larger transactions, consider using a secure payment method.

After the sale, you can mark your ad as “Sold” so others know it's no longer available.

5

Leave Feedback

Help us build a trustworthy community by leaving a review for the other party. This helps others know who to deal with.
