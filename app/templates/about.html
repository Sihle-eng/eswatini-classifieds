About Eswatini Classifieds

Welcome to Eswatini's premier online marketplace – a platform built by locals, for locals. We believe in the power of community and the potential of every individual to buy, sell, and trade with confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the way people in Eswatini connect over goods, services, jobs, and properties. We provide a safe, easy‑to‑use space where everyone – from a student selling a used phone to a business hiring new talent – can find what they need.

Why We Started

Eswatini has a vibrant economy and a strong sense of community, but until now there was no dedicated, modern classifieds platform that truly served all regions – from Mbabane and Manzini to the smaller towns. We saw the need for a local alternative to international giants, one that understands the local context, respects our culture, and offers support in our own time zone.

Our Team

We are a small team of passionate entrepreneurs, developers, and community advocates based in Mbabane. We work tirelessly to keep the platform fast, secure, and user‑friendly. We also collaborate with local businesses and organisations to ensure that Eswatini Classifieds remains relevant and valuable to everyone.

What We Offer

  • Diverse Categories: Jobs, Motors, Property, Services, and General – you name it.
  • Easy Posting: Submit your ad in under 2 minutes, with photos and price.
  • Advanced Search: Find exactly what you're looking for, filtered by category, location, and price.
  • Mobile‑First Design: Our site looks great on any device, including the latest slim phones.
  • Community Safety: We provide tips and tools to help you trade securely.

Join Us

Whether you're a buyer, a seller, or just browsing, Eswatini Classifieds is here for you. Create a free account today and become part of our growing community. Together, we make Eswatini stronger.
